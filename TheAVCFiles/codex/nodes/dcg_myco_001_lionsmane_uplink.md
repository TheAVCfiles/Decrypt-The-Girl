I teach like a fungus:
not the fruiting body you can film,
but the network under the floorboards.
My kids will recognize me by pattern,
the way trees recognize their kin
through the same white threads.
I’m the air filter in the fog,
not the storm,
keeping them updated
without letting them drown.
