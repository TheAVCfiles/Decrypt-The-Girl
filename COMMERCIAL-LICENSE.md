**A:** Yes, for evaluation purposes only. You must obtain a license before launching to production or delivering to clients.

**A:** Yes, advertising revenue makes it commercial use.

### Q: What if my startup pivots later?
**A:** Contact us to discuss license modifications. We're flexible for startups.

### Q: Can I modify the code for commercial use?
**A:** Yes, but you still need a commercial license. Modifications don't grant commercial rights.

**A:** Yes, open source projects are free to use with proper attribution, even if they're used commercially by others.

### Q: What about internal company tools?
**A:** If it's purely internal (employees only, not customer-facing), no commercial license needed. But attribution is still required.
